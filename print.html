Overview

What is 340B?

340B allows eligible hospitals and clinics to buy certain outpatient drugs at discounted prices and reinvest savings into patient care and community services.

HAP position: Protect 340B and contract pharmacy partnerships to preserve access in rural and underserved communities.

21 states have enacted contract pharmacy protection; 29 remain without enacted protection.

What lawmakers can do

  • Protect the 340B discount so hospitals can keep reinvesting in care.
  • Defend contract pharmacy partnerships so patients can fill prescriptions locally.
  • Oppose policies that shrink access for safety-net patients.
