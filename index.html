Why this matters

340B needs attention

Patient access is at risk: manufacturer restrictions and uneven oversight threaten a program that costs taxpayers nothing and helps safety-net providers.

  • Manufacturers limit contract pharmacy access.
  • Only 21 states have contract pharmacy protection laws.
  • Provider audits far outnumber manufacturer audits.

HAP supports 340B integrity and contract pharmacy access for Pennsylvania hospitals and the patients they serve.

The problem

Access at risk

Some manufacturers limit hospitals to one contract pharmacy. Rural and underserved patients depend on contract and specialty pharmacies for high-cost drugs.

Pennsylvania

PA safeguards: no duplicate discounts

  • Manufacturers need not give both a 340B discount and a Medicaid rebate on the same drug.
  • DHS maintains the 340B Drug Exclusion List and invoices manufacturers; covered entities may not dispense 340B-priced drugs on the list.
  • Contract pharmacies use non-340B drugs for MA beneficiaries; DHS invoices manufacturers for those rebates.
